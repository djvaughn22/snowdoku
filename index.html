How to play
  • Every tile is two puzzles at once — the numeral and the sport both have to fit. Pick a card below, then tap where it goes.
  • Green: the bunny slope — I, II, III and IV in four different suits. Every card fits anywhere; just learn to pick up and place. The rules kick in on blue.
  • Blue: 4×4 — every row, column and 2×2 box needs each numeral I–IV and each sport once.
  • Black diamond: 9×9 — full sudoku with numerals I–IX AND nine winter sports, both sudoku-valid at once.
  • Double black: Slopestyle — athletes ski to their tiles. 🎿 drop straight down, 🏂 carve diagonally off walls, ⛸️ hockey skates glide in from the side, 🥾 walk anywhere. Colored lanes show where each entry lands. Undo is free — the terrain is the trap.
  • The lineup is exactly the right cards to finish the board, and only one finished mountain exists — so every move can be figured out, sudoku-style, never guessed. Start where a row, column or box already has the most cards: somewhere, a cell will accept exactly one of your cards.
  • A move that breaks the rules — or would make the mountain impossible to finish — gets an ✗, but the card comes back to you to try somewhere else. Mistakes add up: three ✗ in one run and you’re out. Every deal is solvable.
  • 💡 is your coach — it glows a correct card and spot when you’re stuck.
  • Your game saves itself — close the app, come back, keep climbing.

How to play
  • The goal is survival: the run ends when the mountain fills to the top. Merging keeps it clear — and scores.
  • Tap a ▼ lane (or anywhere in a column) to drop the tile there before the clock drops it for you somewhere random.
  • Touching tiles with equal numerals merge upward: I+I→II … IX+IX→X. Two X’s vanish in a 💥 for a big bonus.
  • Same-sport merges score double, and every point is multiplied by your level.
  • Every 15 drops = next level — the clock gets a little faster. Keep the rhythm.
