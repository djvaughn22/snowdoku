How to play
  • Every tile is two puzzles at once — the numeral and the sport both have to fit. Pick a card below, then tap where it goes.
  • 🟢 Green: the bunny slope — I, II, III and IV in four different suits. Every card fits anywhere; just learn to pick up and place. The rules kick in on blue.
  • 🔵 Blue: 4×4 — every row, column and 2×2 box needs each numeral I–IV and each sport once.
  • ⚫ Black diamond: 9×9 — full sudoku with numerals I–IX AND nine winter sports, both sudoku-valid at once.
  • ⚫⚫ Double black: Slopestyle — athletes ski to their tiles. 🎿 drop straight down, 🏂 carve diagonally off walls, ⛸️ glide in from the side, 🥾 walk anywhere. Colored lanes show where each entry lands. Undo is free — the terrain is the trap.
  • The lineup is exactly the right cards to finish the board. A move that breaks the rules — or would make the mountain impossible to finish — gets an ✗, but the card comes back to you to try somewhere else. Mistakes add up: three ✗ in one run and you’re out. Every deal is solvable.
  • 💡 is your coach — it glows a correct card and spot when you’re stuck.

Tap a column to drop.
How to play
  • Tap a column to drop the next numeral. Equal neighbours merge upward: I+I→II … IX+IX→X. Two X’s vanish for a big bonus.
  • Same-suit merges score double and charge that sport’s power (3 charges).
  • 🎿 clears a column · ⛸️ clears a row · 🏂 clears an X shape · 🥾 clears one tile.
  • The run ends when the board fills up.
